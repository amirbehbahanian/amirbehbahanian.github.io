About Me
I'm Amir Behbahanian, a Software Engineer and Machine Learning Model Developer based in Salt Lake City, Utah. My expertise lies in developing models for tabular data, Semantic Segmentation, and Time-Series analysis. Currently, I work as a Data Scientist/Software Engineer at T.D. Williamson, where I focus on developing advanced models for pipeline component identification, and anomaly detection and characterization. Prior to this, I served as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Texas A&M University, leading a team of Ph.D. students in developing machine learning models for predicting material properties. I've also earned a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Utah State University, where I was recognized as an Outstanding Doctoral Student Researcher. My passion lies in leveraging data-driven approaches to solve complex problems. I'm committed to pushing the boundaries of these fields to make a meaningful impact.
